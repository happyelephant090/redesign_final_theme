Welcome to Redesign!

Project Introduction

    The goal of this assignment is to practice the workflow of redesigning a simple website. One should take away the skills necessary to analyze and identify flaws in an existing interface, create low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes for various screen sizes, and build a responsive website based on those prototypes.

Chosen Website

  • After looking through multiple websites, I chose a website for a gating and fencing business which sells various types of fences and gates, railings, and gate amenities.
